ROTATION REFERENCE

Standard CSS rotation directions:

  • 0deg = No rotation (base image)
  • 90deg = 90° clockwise (right becomes down)
  • 180deg = 180° rotation (right becomes left)
  • 270deg or -90deg = 90° counter-clockwise (right becomes up)
